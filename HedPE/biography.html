  • M.C.U.D. (Jared Gomes)


    • lead vocals, melodica (1994–present)

    Paulo Sergio "Jared" Gomes, also known as Jahred and M.C.U.D. ("MC Underdog") was born on February 29, 1964. He is an American singer, musician and social activist, famous as the frontman of the California punk rock and rap fusion group Hed PE. He also pursued a solo career, releasing a 2012 single titled "Doomsday Paradise" under the stage name of Jahred. During the 1980s, he performed with a dance music group called The Clue. He went on to form Hed (Higher Education) and later changed the group's name to Hed PE, or (hed) Planet Earth. He contributed to a 2007 track by the hip-hop group Twiztid. He grew up in Huntington Beach, California, in a family of Brazilian and African heritage.
